Dr. Laurel Lawyer

Laurie runs the meetings and keeps an eye on everybody. It has been suggested that she is a 'vicious' editor of drafts. She has been known to agree to too many projects, and assign too much work.

Stephanie Aedo Vega

Currently working on language disorders in bilingual Spanish-English children. Stephanie excels at finding and testing an astonishing number of participants.

Ehsan Solaimani Dahanesari

Focusing on syntactic processing in high proficiency L2 speakers of English. Ehsan has interests in syntactic theory, L2 processing, and ultimate attainment. He will at some point be forced to teach us Persian.

Xiaoduan Fan

Researching the processing and perception of gendered language and stereotypes in Mandarin Chinese, her interests span psycholinguistics and sociolinguistics. She can be counted on to remind her lab-mates that not all reading is alphabetic.

Fate O'Gara

"Situation models".

Fiona Soliman

With her background in translation, Fiona approaches language processing and bilingualism from a novel angle. She also speaks at least 15 unrelated languages fluently.

Concepcion Soto Garcia-Melendez

Working on language attrition and heritage language speakers, Inma is a world expert in eye-tracking and making eye-wateringly complicated models.

Willem van Boxtel

A majority of Willem's work focuses on language processing in older populations, and he has keen interests in both normal ageing and Alzheimer's. He is also well-known in the lab for getting too much done too quickly.
