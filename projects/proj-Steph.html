--- title: Language switching in bilingual lexical access icon: fa-archive order: 3 cover-photo: assets/images/proj-Steph.jpg hide: true layout: page --- What happens when a bilingual is forced to switch languages in the middle of a task?

In a project lead by Stephanie Aedo Vega, we use behavioral and EEG measures to look at the processing costs associated with language switching in a lexical access paradigm with Spanish-English bilinguals.
